Rubbish Job Opening

Aug 27th, 2010

Received an email today about a job opening at a major CMM level something company. Required skill : VMWare. Experience required 2 - 15 years. VMWare was founded in 1998, that gives us 12 years since it’s been around. How does one have 15 years of experience with something that wasn’t around until 12 years ago? Who writes these job descriptions?
